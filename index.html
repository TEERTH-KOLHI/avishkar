// FAQ

Your project is handled directly by our senior frontend developers — not outsourced, not delegated to juniors. You get experienced engineers who care about craft.

You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time. There are no lock-ins, minimum commitments, or cancellation fees. Your subscription simply won't renew the following month.

We specialize in frontend implementation and animation — not blank-canvas brand design. We need designs or a design system to work from. That said, we can work closely with your existing design team.

You submit requests via Slack, email, or your preferred tool. We work through them one at a time in your priority order. You can update, add, or reprioritize requests anytime.

Most requests are delivered within 2–5 business days. More complex builds may take longer, but we'll always set clear expectations upfront and communicate proactively.

We're stack-agnostic but specialize in React, Next.js, TypeScript, GSAP, and Framer Motion. We also work with Webflow, Shopify, and WordPress for the right use cases.
